Introduction

Who am I?

Frank M. Taylor. A front-end developer who dabbles in Tridion

I work at Tahzoo worldwide writing front-end code whose final destination is Tridion. I translate PSDs into markup, write jQuery plugins, build frameworks, and generally tinker with the web.

Why are we here?

I'ts not just about the end-user!

As implementers of content management systems, we're actually developing for two audiences Our job is to strike a balance between each audience. And it's a difficult job I'm here because, as a front-end developer who works with CMS developers, I work hard to write code that performs on the website, without making the content author learn a lot of crazy dance moves.
